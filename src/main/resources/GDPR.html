Right to be Informed

We have published our documentation regarding GDPR compliance on this web page. We will inform you of any and all processing activities and their affect on your personal information.

Right to Access to Personal Information

The only personal information we store that is associated with you is the information that you voluntarily provide us with through Elixir AAI authentication and authorization mechanism. This includes: your ELIXIR identifier, name and e-mail address. We also store logs containing IP addresses but these logs are not associated with a single user account.

Right to Rectification of Personal Information

You can request this via e-mail to nels-developers@googlegroups.com

Right to Erasure

You can request this via e-mail to nels-developers@googlegroups.com

Right to Restrict Processing

All of the processing activities we currently do are required to use the service. We do not transmit any PII to third parties.

Right to Data Portability

You can request this via e-mail to nels-developers@googlegroups.com

Right to Object

All of the processing activities we currently do are required to use the service. We do not transmit any PII to third parties. If we do processing activities in the future that do affect PII, we will inform you and permit you to opt-out.

Right to Not be Subject to Automated Decision-making Including Profiling

We do not profile our users, nor do any form of automated decision making based on personal data.
